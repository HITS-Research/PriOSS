Note: It is not guaranteed that all ads related to each category can be avoided completely. Ads from certain categories like gambling and dating are restricted by default.

Here’s how you can prevent and block specific ad categories and advertiser accounts to appear on your profile feed or reels:


Step 1- First of all, you have to visit Instagram website on your computer or mobile browser or you can open Instagram application on your mobile phone.



Step 2- In the Instagram app, go to your Settings.



Step 3- Now click on Account Center.



Step 4- Choose the option Ad preferences.



Step 5- Now click on option Ad Topics.



Step 6- Now click on View and manage topics.



Step 7- Now you can sort and filter the advertisements.

Here’s how you can prevent and block specific ad categories and advertiser accounts to appear on your profile feed or reels:


Step 1 - While viewing your Instagram feed, tap on your profile icon in the bottom right corner of the screen.



Step 2 - Tap the menu icon in the upper right corner of your profile.



Step 3 - Tap the Settings and privacy gear icon in the menu that appears.



Step 4- Now click on Account Center.



Step 5 - Choose the option Ad preferences.



Step 6 - Now click on option Ad Topics.



Step 7 - Now click on View and manage topics.



Step 8 - Now you can sort and filter the advertisements.
