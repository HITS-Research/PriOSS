Do not expose too much private data in your profile information and especially in your EDIT PROFILE area.

Do not write any personal information that could be used against you in the Bio field.

Follow these steps if you want to keep your profile information private for your Instagram account on your computer or mobile browser-


Step 1 - First of all, you have to visit Instagram website on your computer or mobile browser.



Step 2 - You need to click profile icon.



Step 3 - Now, click on Edit Profile option.



Step 4 - Now, you can edit your information in 'Bio' section.

To only share your photos with the users you choose, like your friends and family, follow these steps:


Step 1 - Go to your Instagram profile window and click edit your profile.
