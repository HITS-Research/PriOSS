If you don’t want your photos to be public and available to be seen by everyone, you can make your profile private.


Follow these steps if you want to set your Instagram account to private on your computer or mobile browser-


Step 1 - First of all, you have to visit Instagram website on your computer or mobile browser.

Step 2 – You need to click profile icon and then, click settings.

Step 3 – Here, you have to click Privacy and Security.

Step 4 – Below Account Privacy, you need to click to check the box next to Private Account.

Following these steps can help you to set your Instagram account to private on your computer or mobile browser.

To only share your photos with the users you choose, like your friends and family, follow these steps:


Step 1 - Go to your Instagram profile window.

Step 2 – Click on the three dots in the right corner.

Step 3 – At the bottom you’ll find the Private Account option. Make sure this security setting is ON. (The button should turn to Blue).
Activating this option means that only those who follow you can see your online photos.
