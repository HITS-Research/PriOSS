If you don’t want your photos to be public and available to be seen by everyone, you can make your profile private.


Step 1 - Open your Instagram app on your mobile device. Tap on your profile picture in the bottom right to go to your profile, then tap (3 bars icon) in the top right.

Step 2 – Look for the gear symbol and go in Settings.

Step 3 – Move through the next screens by tapping Security > Two-factor authentication > Get Started.

Step 4 – You'll need to choose your security method. Select Authentication app(recommended). Then choose Next.

Step 5 – Authenticator App will automatically choose the Instagram logo and autofill account name and secret key. Confirm it's correct by clicking Save.

Step 6 – Copy the numerical token provided for Instagram. Note that this code refreshes every 30 seconds.

Step 7 - Return to the Instagram app and type the numerical token in the appropriate field. When done tap Next.

Step 8 - Your Instagram account is now protected. Press on Next, you are presented with access to your backup codes. Each code can only be used once. We suggest you to store them digitally in a safe place. Press Done and that's all.
