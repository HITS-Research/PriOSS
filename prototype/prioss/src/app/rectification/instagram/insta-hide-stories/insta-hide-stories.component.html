You should secure your privacy not just for photos or location, but also for your stories.


It’s easy to hide Instagram Stories from specific individuals or deny people the chance to share your stories with their followers.


Here’s how to hide Stories or control who interacts with them:


Step 1 - While viewing your Instagram feed, tap on your profile icon in the bottom right corner of the screen.

Step 2 – Tap the menu icon in the upper right corner of your profile.

Step 3 – Tap the Settings gear icon in the menu that appears.

Step 4 – Select Privacy option.

Step 5 – Tap Story option.

Step 6 – Underneath the heading "Hide Story From", select "# People."

Step 7 – On Androids, tap the arrow in the upper left corner of your screen when you have finished adding users to the list. iPhone users should select "Done" in the top right.
