Guidelines to turn OFF Off-Facebook Activity

Step 1 - Click on Account on the top right corner of the page.

Step 2 – Click on Privacy and Settings.

Step 3 – Click on Settings.

Step 4 – Navigate to Your Facebook Information.

Step 5 – Click on Off–Facebook Activity.


After this follow
Step 6 If you want to select and disconnect specific future activities
Step 7 If you want to clear previous activitiy
Step 8 and 9 If you want to turn OFF Off-Facebook Activity

Step 6 – Click on Select future activity to disconnect and put your Facebook password to select the activities you want to disconnect.

Step 7 – Click on Clear previous activity and confirm again by clicking clear.

Step 8 – Click on Manage future activity.

Step 9 – Click on Disconnect future activity.

Step 5 If you want to clear previous activitiy
