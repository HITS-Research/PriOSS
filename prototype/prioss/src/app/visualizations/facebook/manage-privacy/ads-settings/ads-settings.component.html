Step 1 - Click on Account on the top right corner of the page.

Step 2 – Click on Privacy and Settings.

Step 3 – Click on Settings.

Step 4 – Navigate to Your Facebook Information and then click on view in front of Access your Information.

Step 5 – Click on Ads Information and then click on Advertisers you've interacted with.

Step 6 – Choose the advertiser whose ads you want to hide and click hide ads.
