Welcome to the dashboard for the Instagram specific data. This page will offer you all the information you need, to fully understand the data that is collected about you and what steps you can take to increase your personal privacy.

In this section you will be presented with different visualizations of the data you put in before. Every card shows a preview of the data from a specific topic. By clicking on 'More' you will be redirected to a page with a more precise visualization and description of the specific topic.
