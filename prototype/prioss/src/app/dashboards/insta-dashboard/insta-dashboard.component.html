Rectification

This section of the application describes how a user can rectify if something is missing/ wrong in the data download.

  1. Go into the settings of Instagram and report a problem.
  2. Send an email to support@instagram.com. Generally, Instagram team responds within a week.
  3. Contact the privacy center for Meta. It covers Facebook, Instagram, Messenger, and other Meta products.

GDPR/Rights

This section describes the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Is a rule in EU law that ensures a natural person's rights to data protection and privacy, independent of their nationality or place of residence, and upholds fundamental rights to secure their personal data stored by various companies. To comply with GDPR standards, companies offer data downloads or download- able archives.

Purpose

This section describes the purpose of our application. We are providing a privacy-focused application that will help users to exercise their General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) rights. Also helping them to rectify and get additional context information and recommendations about their personal data collected by Instagram.
